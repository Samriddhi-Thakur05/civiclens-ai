# CivicLens AI Demo Script

## 90-second flow

1. Open with the problem:
   "Local civic issues like potholes, damaged streetlights, and waste overflow are easy to report informally but hard to verify, prioritize, route, and track transparently."

2. Show the report screen:
   "CivicLens AI lets a citizen upload a field photo, add the area and landmark, and describe what people nearby are experiencing."

3. Use the sample:
   Click **Load sample**, then **Analyze & create case**.

4. Explain the AI triage:
   "Gemini converts the unstructured report into an operational case: category, severity, priority score, likely department, duplicate risk, reasoning, and next actions."

5. Add it to the tracker:
   Click **Add to public tracker**.

6. Show community validation:
   "Residents can confirm the issue. Once enough confirmations come in, the case becomes verified and rises in credibility."

7. Show status progress:
   Click **Advance** to move a case through Reported, Verified, Assigned, In Progress, and Resolved.

8. Show the dashboard:
   "The dashboard gives officials and citizens a transparent view of open cases, high-priority cases, confirmations, resolved cases, category split, and local issue density."

9. Close with impact:
   "This is not just a complaint form. It is an AI civic triage agent that helps communities convert field evidence into prioritized, trackable action."

## Best judge-facing line

CivicLens AI uses Google AI Studio and Gemini to transform citizen-submitted images and notes into structured civic cases that can be validated by the community, routed to responsible departments, and tracked publicly until resolution.
